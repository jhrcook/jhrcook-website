## Introduction

After enjoying a few of the Bond novels that I picked up at a used bookstore in Redding, CA, I decided to read the full series.
With one book remaining to read at the beginning of 2026, Netflix brokered an agreement with Netflix to have all of the Eon Production Bond movies available to stream.
So as I wrap up one medium, I'm transitioning to the other and figured it worthwhile to rank and comment on the novels and movies as I go.
